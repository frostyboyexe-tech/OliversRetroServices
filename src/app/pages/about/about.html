About Oliver

Hi! I'm Oliver! The founder of ORS (Oliver's Retro Services). I've always been fascinated by electronics and how they work. Over the years, I've spent countless hours researching and learning about how technology works. I enjoy figuring out how everything connects and works together.

Programming and video games have also been a major passion of mine. I’ve spent years creating my own games — including retro-style games designed to run on real hardware. Working within the limitations of these systems has taught me a lot about efficiency, optimization, and how these consoles operate.

Recently, I've become deeply interested in retro consoles and electrical engineering. So, I started ORS to fufill my passion and to help bring the passion to others. Working on retro consoles combines everything I love -- electronics, programming knowledge, video games, and hands-on problem solving.

With Oliver's Retro Services I wish to share my passions with my local community by giving you a reliable, forward, and trustworthy service.

What Does ORS Do?

ORS has mutliple services depending on your need. We provide custom consoles, repairs, restorations, and more! Whether you need a childhood memory restored to its original glory, or you want to discover new games on a brand new Gameboy, ORS is there to help.
